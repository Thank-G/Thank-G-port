TREASUREHALL TRAINING is an off-shoot of TREASUREHALL CONSULTING which was registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in 2001 as a full fledged financial and management consulting firm.

Treasurehall Training Treasurehall is a Corporate member of The Nigerian Institute of Training and Development (NITAD). It is accredited by The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) for pre-induction training of its members. They provide broad-based platform to improve and enhance the knowledge and professional skills of its trainees. As a professional training centre dedicated to best practice in management and development, they continually update their facilities and knowledge base in order to give its trainees the best and most current knowledge and skills.
